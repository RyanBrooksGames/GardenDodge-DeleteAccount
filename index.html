Garden Dodge Account Deletion Process

If you would like your account associated with this game please follow the below steps:

1. Open Garden Dodge

2. Go into the leaderboard and make a note of your Display name. This can be done by pressing the "Around Player" button in the top right corner. Your display name will appear in blue.

3. Go into the settings, click on the Reveal Player ID button in the bottom left corner, and make a note of your player ID.

4. Send an email to RyanBrooksGames@gmail.com with the subject: Account Deletion Request, and in the body include your display name and player ID.

5. Within the next few days after the email is received, your account should be deleted.
